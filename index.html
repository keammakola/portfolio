About me

I’m Keabetswe Mmakola, a Gauteng-based engineer who lives at the intersection of code and clarity. By combining software, data engineering, and data science, I turn messy, fragmented data into a roadmap for business growth. I build the tools that make "data-driven" actually mean something.

My expertise spans the entire data lifecycle - from building robust ETL pipelines and cloud infrastructure to developing machine learning models and creating compelling visualisations. I'm particularly passionate about sharing knowledge through public speaking and educational content creation.

Away from the screen, you’ll find me mentoring new developers, contributing to the open-source community, or creating tutorials for my YouTube channel, Study Squad SA, to help high schoolers navigate complex topics taught at school.
